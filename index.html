What is the Almond Council of Central Wisconson?

The ACCW was founded October 2016 to help spread educate central Wisconsin Citizens about the wonderful and amazing Almond. Located in small town of Almond we have worked to get the word out all the wonderful health benifits of almonds. Please stop by one our many kiosks located around central Wisconsin for free samples and a list of upcomming events.
